Why AI Training for Everyone?

Artificial intelligence is no longer a specialist subject reserved for data scientists and engineers. It is reshaping how every function works — from the shop floor to the boardroom. As part of AI Awareness Month, this session opens the door for everyone to understand what AI really is, how it works, and where it already touches our daily work.

No prior technical background is required. Whether you are an operator, an executive, or a support-function team member, you will leave with a clear, practical understanding of AI and the confidence to start applying it.

What You Will Learn

  • The fundamentals of artificial intelligence and machine learning, explained in plain language.
  • Real-world examples of AI across Safety, Quality, Productivity, Cost, Delivery, Digital, Morale & Environment (SQPCDDME).
  • How everyday tools — chat assistants, automation, and analytics — can save time and reduce effort.
  • Practical first steps to bring AI into your own role and team.

Who Should Attend

Two dedicated sessions run on the same day so everyone can join the one that fits best:

  • AI Training for All — open to all employees who want a strong foundation in AI (morning session).
  • AI for Group-IT & Corporate — a focused virtual meet for IT and corporate teams (afternoon session).

How to Join

Sessions will be held live with industry experts. Use the Join Now link to register and add the session to your calendar. The virtual meeting link for the Group-IT & Corporate session is available in the schedule card alongside.
