What each session covers

Two focused two-hour tracks, each built around five hands-on modules — from the fundamentals of Generative AI to real workflow gains and responsible, secure use. Pick a session below to see its full agenda.

1

Fundamentals of Generative AI

20 min
  • Understanding the core architecture of Large Language Models
  • Core capabilities and limitations of AI generation
  • Generative processing versus traditional search retrieval
  • Transitioning to a delegation-based AI workflow
2

Principles of Effective Prompt Engineering

25 min
  • Foundational prompting strategies for corporate users
  • Applying the Context, Role, Task, Format, Tone framework
  • Live demo: optimizing low-yield prompts into actionable instructions
3

Workflow: Communications & Documentation

30 min
  • Accelerating document review and drafting processes
  • Extracting critical action items from extensive correspondence and reports
  • Refining text tone for professional corporate communications
  • Generating foundational drafts for SOPs and updates
4

Workflow: Data Management & Analysis

30 min
  • Advanced spreadsheet utilization for non-technical users
  • Translating natural language requests into complex spreadsheet formulas
  • Structuring and standardizing raw, unformatted datasets
  • Deconstructing and interpreting legacy spreadsheet logic
5

Data Governance & Risk Management

15 min
  • Protocols for protecting proprietary corporate assets
  • Identifying strictly confidential data types (financials, PII, internal specifications)
  • Techniques for data anonymization prior to AI processing
  • Mitigating AI hallucinations through mandatory human verification
  • Interactive Q&A
